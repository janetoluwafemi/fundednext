How can I participate in the "Refer &

Earn" program?

Updated over 3 months ago

To participate, traders need to have purchased at least one account on FundedNext. Once they’ve made a purchase and passed the waiting period of 7 days, they can go to the "Refer & Earn" section in their FundedNext dashboard and click on the code under “Referral Code” to copy their referral code and share it with their friends and family.
