to CFD (Forex) Trading?

TL;DR: Profit/Loss in futures trading = (Tick Value √ó Ticks Moved) √ó Contracts. More ticks and contracts mean higher profits or losses.

Futures are contracts that let traders agree to buy or sell an asset at a set price, but on a future date. Instead of owning the asset, traders speculate on whether its price will go up or down. This makes it a popular choice for trading commodities like oil, gold, stock indices, and even currencies.

Futures allow traders to either protect their investments or take advantage of market shifts for potential profit. It's a way to gain exposure to big markets without having to actually own the assets.
