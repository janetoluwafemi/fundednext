How to pay for your FundedNext Futures Challenge?

How to pay for your FundedNext

Futures Challenge?

Updated over 2 weeks ago

Purchasing your favorite FundedNext Futures Challenge is simple. Follow these steps to complete your payment:

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Log in to your FundedNext Dashboard and go to the "Start Challenge" section.

Step 2: Select "Futures" and choose your preferred model and size, then click "Get Plan" to proceed.

Step 3: Choose a payment method from the available options displayed.

Step 4: Complete the transaction following the on-screen instructions.

Once the payment is processed, you will receive a confirmation email.
