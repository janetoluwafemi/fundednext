When will I receive my account

credentials after payment?

Updated over 2 weeks ago

  • Instant access: Payments via card & crypto are processed immediately.
  • Slight verification delay: Other payment methods may require additional verification, causing minor delays.

Where can I find my login credentials?

Once your account is successfully created:

  • You will receive an email notification with your login details.
  • You can also find the login credentials in your FundedNext dashboard.

Did you not receive the email?

Check your Spam/Junk folder in case the email was filtered.

Security Reminder:

FundedNext strongly advises you to keep your login credentials secure and never share them with anyone. Protecting your account ensures the safety of your trading activities.
